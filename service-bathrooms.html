1. Consultation & Design: We start with a detailed consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. Our designers will create a comprehensive plan with 3D renders.

2. Planning & Preparation: We handle all necessary building regulations, prepare the space, and organize the supply of all materials and fixtures.
