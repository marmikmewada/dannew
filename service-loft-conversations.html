Loft Conversions

Unlock the potential of your home with a professional loft conversion. Add valuable living space, increase your property value, and create the room you've always dreamed of—all without the hassle of moving.

Expert Loft Conversion Services

A loft conversion is one of the most cost-effective ways to add space and value to your home. At DS Renovations, we specialize in transforming unused attic space into beautiful, functional rooms that perfectly suit your lifestyle and needs.

Types of Loft Conversions We Offer

  • Roof Light Conversions - Most cost-effective option with Velux windows
  • Dormer Conversions - Additional headroom and floor space
  • Hip-to-Gable Conversions - Ideal for semi-detached properties
  • Mansard Conversions - Maximum space for terraced properties
  • Custom Loft Designs - Tailored to your specific requirements

What's Included in Our Service

We provide a complete, end-to-end loft conversion service that takes care of every detail from initial design through to final finishing. Our comprehensive approach means you have a single point of contact throughout the project.

  • Free initial consultation and feasibility assessment
  • Architectural design and planning application support
  • Building regulations approval and certification
  • Structural calculations and engineer reports
  • Full construction work including steelwork
  • Dormer or roof window installation
  • Staircase design and installation
  • Electrical work including lighting and power points
  • Plumbing for en-suite bathrooms
  • Insulation for year-round comfort
  • Plastering and decorating
  • Flooring installation
  • Heating system integration

Popular Loft Conversion Uses

The beauty of a loft conversion is its versatility. We've created stunning spaces for a variety of purposes:

  • Master bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms
  • Home offices and workspaces
  • Guest bedrooms
  • Playrooms and teenage retreats
  • Hobby rooms and creative studios
  • Home cinemas and entertainment spaces
  • Gym and wellness areas

Why Choose DS Renovations?

With years of experience in loft conversions across Staffordshire, we understand the unique challenges each property presents. Our team has successfully converted lofts in Victorian terraces, modern semi-detached homes, and everything in between.

We're proud to say that most of our loft conversions don't require planning permission, though we'll guide you through the process if yours does. All our work fully complies with building regulations, and we provide all necessary certifications upon completion.

Investment and Returns

A professionally executed loft conversion typically adds 10-20% to your property value—often more than the cost of the conversion itself. It's an investment that pays dividends both in increased living space and financial return.
