Expert Plastering for Perfect Finishes

At DS Renovations, our skilled plasterers deliver immaculate finishes for both residential and commercial properties. With decades of combined experience, we specialize in all aspects of plastering, from traditional lime plastering to modern skimming techniques.

Whether you're renovating a period property or building a new home, our plastering services ensure smooth, durable surfaces that stand the test of time.

Our Plastering Services Include:

  • Internal wall and ceiling plastering
  • External rendering and rendering repairs
  • Skimming and re-skimming
  • Artex removal and ceiling restoration
  • Damp-proof plastering solutions
  • Decorative plasterwork and cornicing
  • Fire-damaged wall restoration
  • Period property lime plastering
