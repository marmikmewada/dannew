About DS Renovations

Built on Innovation, Integrity, and Results

We specialise in high-quality renovation and property improvement services tailored to each client’s needs. From carpentry and plastering to full kitchen and bathroom fit-outs, our experienced tradespeople deliver precision, care, and reliability on every project.

Tradesmen That Stand the Test of Time

Based in Rugeley, Staffordshire, we work locally and nationally across the UK. No matter the size or scope of the job, our approach remains the same — professional, reliable, and results-driven.

We provide carpentry, plastering, kitchen fitting, bathroom fitting, painting and decorating, laminate and wood flooring, insurance work, subsidence work, and expert solutions for fire and water damage. Modern techniques, trusted materials, and clear communication keep every project on track and to the highest standard.

Get in touch today for a free, no-obligation quote at info@dsrenovations.co.uk — let’s discuss your project.
